You can also mention the continuous holidays by specifying the <a target='_blank' href='https://help.syncfusion.com/cr/blazor/Syncfusion.Blazor.Gantt.GanttHoliday.html#Syncfusion_Blazor_Gantt_GanttHoliday_From'>From</a> and <a target='_blank' href='https://help.syncfusion.com/cr/blazor/Syncfusion.Blazor.Gantt.GanttHoliday.html#Syncfusion_Blazor_Gantt_GanttHoliday_To'>To</a> range. More than one holiday can be displayed in the project.</p>
    <p>You can even assign the <a target='_blank' href='https://help.syncfusion.com/cr/blazor/Syncfusion.Blazor.Gantt.GanttHoliday.html#Syncfusion_Blazor_Gantt_GanttHoliday_CssClass'>CssClass</a> to each holiday to change the default color of the label and background.</p>
