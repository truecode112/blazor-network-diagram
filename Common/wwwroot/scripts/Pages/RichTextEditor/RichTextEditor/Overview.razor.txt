<ActionDescription>
    <p>The editor’s toolbar contains the below commands to edit and format the content.</p>
    <ul>
        <li><code>Format Text</code> – Options to apply inline styles to the selected content like bold, italic, strikethrough and more.</li>
        <li><code>Font</code> - Option to add variety of colors and styles to the text like font, font size, font color and background color.</li>
        <li><code>Sentence Style</code> – Options to format the sentence style in different ways such as heading level, quotation, and code snippet</li>
        <li><code>Subscript and Superscript</code> - Change the selected text to subscript or superscript.</li>
        <li><code>Clear Formats</code> - Option to clear all formatting (such as bold, underline, italics, color, superscript, subscript, and more).</li>
        <li><code>Undo and Redo</code> - Options to undo and redo operations.</li>
        <li><code>Indent and Outdent</code> - Increases or decreases the indent level of the content.</li>
        <li><code>Change Case</code> –Change the selected text to upper case or lower case.</li>
        <li><code>Alignment</code> - Aligns the content with left, center, and right margins.</li>
        <li><code>Lists</code> - Option to create numbered lists and bulleted lists.</li>
        <li><code>Links</code> - A Hyperlink can be inserted into the editor for quick access to related information.</li>
        <li><code>Image</code> - Inserts and manages images.</li>
        <li><code>Table</code> - Inserts and manages Tables.</li>
        <li><code>Print</code> - Print the content.</li>
        <li><code>Code View</code> - Option to view the HTML source.</li>
    </ul>
</ActionDescription>
